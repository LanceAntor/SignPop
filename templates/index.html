How To play

  1. Position your hands clearly in the camera view with good lighting.
  2. Make the correct sign language gesture for each falling letter.
  3. Pop letters before they hit bottom to score points.
  4. Miss a letter or make the wrong sign? You lose a life!
  5. Speed increases as your score goes up. Good luck!
